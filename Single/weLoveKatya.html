We Love Katya

"We Love Katya" is a Google Chrome extension that replaces several images on the screen for pictures of Yekaterina Petrovna Zamolodchikova, the famous Drag Queen also known as "Katya".

The idea came after the Season 2 of RuPaul's Drag Race - All Stars, when (spoiler alert!) RuPaul got Alaska as the winner. Don't get me wrong, Alaska is a great Drag Queen, but I was a huge fan of Katya and was really hopping that she would win.

It didn't happen, but at least I can promote this amazing performer!

Technologies I used

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Vanilla JavaScript
