Planos de Governo

2018 was the year for the most recent presidential election in Brazil. The two final candidates on the run submitted official transcripts containing what would be the guidelines for country management for the next 4 years in case they got elected.

This dashboard (in PT-BR) shows what is being said in those transcripts, with which frequency and how many times important subjects are referred on these government plans. Also, this dashboard compares how these ideas are reflected in public tweets from each individual candidate.

Technologies I used

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • ChartJS
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Numpy
