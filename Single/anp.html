Gas Prices in Brazil

With data from Brazil's National Agency of Petroleum, Natural Gas and Bio fuels, I built a dashboard to understand how prices of fuels like gas, diesel and ethanol behaved in different parts of the country in the last years.

This project also shows in which week of the month is cheaper to buy gas (or other fuels) depending on where you live in Brazil.

Technologies I used

  • R Studio
  • R Shiny
  • R Markdown
  • Pandas
