NDH

"NDH" is the abbreviation for "Núcleo de Documentação Histórica", or "Center of Historical Documentation", in english.

This project was developed to help the History Department of the Federal University of Pelotas. This Department holds over 100000 working legal processes from the last century.

All this material is being digitalized in order to preserve it's historical and scientific value. With this web application, civil and academic students can make complex searches in order to understand the working reality of those living in Pelotas, RS - Brazil in the XX century.

It is also used by the students who digitalize those processes and it is a way to preserve the history and to make scienfic production easier for future students.

Technologies I used
