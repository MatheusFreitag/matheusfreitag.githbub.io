PET

The PET Group (being PET the short of Tutoring Educational Program) is formed by students of the Computer Science and Computer Engineering courses at the Federal University of Pelotas, in Brazil. The group hosts activities for the general community and the community inside the university, trying to bring the benefits of the knowledge learned in the Academia into the real world.

My goal here was to rebrand and adjust their website in order to guarantee a pleasent and solid experience when sharing the news, events, schedules and any kind of information they needed.

I was responsible for every details of the website. I designed, prototyped, coded, tested and hosted the whole system, and now it is a key product to help the group in all their activities.

Technologies I used

  • JavaScript
